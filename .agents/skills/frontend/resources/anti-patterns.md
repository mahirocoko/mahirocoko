# Anti-Patterns

- Do not place tests in route discovery folders
- Do not bypass type safety with `any` as a quick fix
- Do not combine conflicting lint/format toolchains
- Do not keep multiple lockfiles in one repository
- Do not mix service access directly into reusable presentational components
- Do not duplicate auth/session source of truth across many unrelated modules
- Do not bypass established i18n flow by hardcoding mixed-language UI copy without Lingui
