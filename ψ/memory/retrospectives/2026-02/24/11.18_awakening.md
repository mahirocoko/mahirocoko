1. The trace skill may need more specific queries than "oracle philosophy principles"
2. Background tasks with 5 agents might timeout or hang
3. The skill-to-skill invocation might not work as expected

For future awakenings, I would suggest:
- Having fallback sources ready (the skill docs themselves contain the principles)
